Trinkets

Cat Eye Trinket

Effect: Permanently increases the length of Fortifying tonic effects by 20%

Location: Flat Iron Lake, in a lockbox on the southernmost island near Braithwaite Manor.

Crow Beak Trinket

Effect: Permanently increases looted ammo by 10%

Location: Coot's Chapel, the final reward of The Elemental Trail treasure map chain.

Eagle Talon Talisman

Effect: Raises Eagle Eye duration by five seconds.

Location: Player inventory, Special/Ultimate edition.

Hawk Talon Trinket

Effect: Permanently decreases Stamina bar drain speed by 30% when drawing a bow.

Location: Deadboot Creek, in a lockbox at a destroyed campsite near the top of the creek.

Iguana Scale Trinket

Effect: Decreases damage player receives while on horseback by 10%

Location: Player inventory, Special/Ultimate edition.

Owl Feather Trinket

Effect: Lowers Health, Stamina and Dead Eye core drain speed by 15%

Location: Given by Rains Fall Kill no one during "Archeology for Beginners" mission.

Shark Tooth Trinket

Effect: Permanently increases horse bonding experience bonus by 10%

Location: Roanoke Ridge, in a lockbox near a beached ship east of Annesburg.

Turtle Shell Trinket

Effect: Permanently increases health bar refill speed by 10%

Location: Gaptooth Breach, in a lockbox under a set of stairs connected to the largest building of the complex.
